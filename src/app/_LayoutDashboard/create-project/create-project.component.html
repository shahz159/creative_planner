Core Task

All the major projects or tasks that contributes to the development of concerned department or organization such as new products, new store opening system development, trainings, etc.

Client:

The team member has to select the client's name to whom the project belongs to.

Project Name:

Project Name should be more than 3 words and Please try to avoid using special characters like @ # $ % & * ! ( ) ; , . . . .Etc.

Ex: Enhancement of Transportation and Distribiution Division through GPS Technology

Project Description:

Project Description should be more than 5 words and Please try to avoid using special characters like -@ # $ % & * ! ( ) ; , . . . .Etc.

Ex: The task is created on the advice from the President to enhance the transportation & distribution division through GPS technology and developing transportation KPIs tracking report on weekly & monthly basis

Category:

Project belongs to which type of category.

Team Section:
R

Responsible, The Team Member who is totally responsible for the successful completion of the undertaken project type and also its sub-tasks on or before the agreed deadline.

A

Authority, The Authorized Team Member who only can execute any kind Modifications, Alterations and Changes by his own or through the superiors instruction or direction.

C

Coordinating, The team member who always available to provide any kind of details of the project and communication and coordination with other departments or companies for the smooth execution of the project along with the project responsible Team member.

I

Inform, The direct superior of the project responsible team member or the team member who continuously receives the details or the information regarding the project from the project responsible person.

S

Support, The team member who supports the project responsible team member and provides him with all kind of technical, documentation, content supports etc.

Sub Section:

select Starting Date & Time and the expected Ending Date & Time of the given project. A team member cannot select the DPG (Date of Project Given) which is less than the current date. You should not select the past date as a deadline. System will not accept.

Reference:

Please attach any reference document of the given project.

Subtask :

a step by step process to complete the project. Each subtask have name , description, start Date, End Date, TotalDuration time with multiple owner selection(optional)

Do you want to save as Template :

If you select then this project will add in templates list.

Template :

You can re use previous projects structure for new projects
